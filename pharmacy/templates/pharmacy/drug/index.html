Drug Product Helper

**Drug Products** are the actual inventory items you manage. Each product is linked to a **Formulation** (e.g., Paracetamol 500mg Tablet) and a **Manufacturer**.

Tip: You can track quantities in the **Store/Warehouse** and at the **Pharmacy Counter** separately. Set a **Minimum Stock Level** to receive alerts for low stock.
Note: The **SKU (Stock Keeping Unit)** must be unique for each drug product.
