Rehabilitation & Physiotherapy Services

Our physiotherapy department provides personalised rehabilitation programs to help patients recover mobility, reduce pain, and regain independence after injury, surgery or illness. We combine evidence-based therapy, modern equipment, and compassionate therapists to deliver measurable results.

Whether you are recovering from orthopaedic surgery, managing chronic pain, or need neurologic rehabilitation, our multidisciplinary team will design a plan tailored to your goals.

Who Can Benefit

Patients with sports injuries, post-operative recovery needs, stroke or neurological conditions, chronic back/neck pain, and mobility limitations will benefit from our targeted physiotherapy programs.

Therapies & Facilities

  • Manual Therapy & Mobilisation
  • Exercise Therapy & Functional Training
  • Electrotherapy & Modalities
  • Hydrotherapy & Gait Training
  • Home Exercise Programs
