Comprehensive Surgical Care

Our surgical services provide safe, evidence-based operative care across a broad range of specialties. Our surgical teams include experienced consultants, anaesthetists and nursing staff supported by modern operating theatres and perioperative units to ensure excellent outcomes.

We offer both elective and emergency surgery, with dedicated pre-operative assessment and post-operative recovery services to support fast, safe recoveries.

Surgical Specialties

Our surgical department covers general surgery, orthopaedics, ENT, ophthalmic surgery, gynaecology, and minor day-case procedures. Complex cases are handled by multidisciplinary teams with access to advanced diagnostics and critical care support.

Operating Theatres & Recovery

  • Modern, fully-equipped operating theatres
  • Dedicated anaesthesia and critical care teams
  • Post-anesthesia care and high-dependency units
  • Enhanced recovery pathways
  • Minimally invasive and laparoscopic techniques

Pre-Op & Post-Op Care

Before surgery, our pre-operative team will assess your fitness, review medications, and explain the procedure and recovery expectations. After surgery you will be monitored in recovery and given a clear discharge and rehabilitation plan tailored to your needs.

Frequently Asked Questions

Your surgical team will provide pre-op instructions which may include fasting, medication adjustments, and arrival time. If you have questions, contact the pre-operative clinic or your surgeon.

We offer multimodal pain management including regional blocks, oral and IV medication, and individualized plans to keep you comfortable during recovery.

Length of stay varies by procedure. Day-case and minor procedures often go home the same day; major procedures may require overnight or longer stays. Your surgeon will discuss anticipated timelines.
