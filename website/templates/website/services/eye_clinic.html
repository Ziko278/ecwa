Comprehensive Ophthalmology & Eye Care

Our Eye Clinic offers a full range of ophthalmic services, from routine eye exams and vision correction to advanced surgical procedures. We combine experienced ophthalmologists with modern diagnostic equipment to provide precise, patient-centred care.

Whether you need cataract surgery, glaucoma management, retinal care or routine optical services, our multidisciplinary team works to preserve and restore vision.

Services Offered

We provide diagnostic assessments, medical and surgical treatment, refractive services, paediatric ophthalmology and low-vision support. Our clinic also offers on-site optical dispensing for glasses and contact lens fitting.

When to See an Ophthalmologist

Book an appointment if you experience sudden vision changes, eye pain, flashes/floaters, or if you have a family history of eye disease. Regular eye checks are recommended for patients with diabetes, hypertension, or those over 40 years old.
