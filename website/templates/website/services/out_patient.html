Accessible, Efficient Out-Patient Care

Our Out-Patient clinics provide same-day consultations, follow-ups and minor procedures with minimal waiting times. We focus on delivering prompt, evidence-based care across multiple specialties so you can receive the treatment you need without admission.

From routine check-ups to specialist consultations, our outpatient services are designed for convenience and quality — including on-site diagnostics and pharmacy services to reduce your number of visits.

How Out-Patient Visits Work

When you arrive for an outpatient appointment, you will be registered at reception and guided to the appropriate clinic. Consultations may include physical exams, diagnostic tests, or brief procedures that do not require overnight admission. Our staff will provide clear aftercare instructions and follow-up plans.
