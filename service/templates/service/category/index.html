Category Helper Guide

A Service Category is a high-level grouping for all financial activities in the hospital. Examples include: 'Laboratory', 'Pharmacy', 'Consultation', 'Optical Sales'.

CATEGORY TYPE: This defines what can be added to the category.

  • Service/Procedure: For abstract services like consultations or lab tests.
  • Item/Product: For physical goods that have stock, like drugs or eyeglass frames.
  • Both Services and Items: For categories that can contain both types.

SHOW ON RECORD: If checked, this category might be given its own dedicated column on certain financial reports or detailed patient statements, making specific charges easier to see.

Tip: Proper categorization is the foundation of good financial tracking and reporting.
