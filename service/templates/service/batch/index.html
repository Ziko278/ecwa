Item Batch Helper

An Item Batch is used to track a single purchase or delivery of multiple inventory items. It acts like a digital invoice or goods received note.

Workflow: First, you create a new batch. Then, you click "Add Stock" to go to a separate page where you can add all the individual items that belong to that batch.
Note: You can only edit or delete the **most recently created batch**. Once a new batch is created, older ones are locked for auditing purposes.
