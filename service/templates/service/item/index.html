Inventory Item Helper Guide

An Item is a physical product that you sell or dispense to patients. Unlike services, items have stock levels that must be managed.

STOCK QUANTITY: The current number of units available in your inventory.

MINIMUM STOCK LEVEL: When the quantity on hand falls to this number or below, it will be flagged as "Low Stock". This is a trigger to re-order the item.

COST PRICE vs. SELLING PRICE: You can record both what you paid for the item (Cost Price) and what you sell it for (Selling Price) to track profitability.

Tip: The system automatically deducts stock when an item is marked as paid on a patient's bill. To add new stock, you must use the "Manage Stock" feature on the item's detail page.
