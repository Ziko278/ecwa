Service Helper Guide

A Service is a non-physical procedure or consultation offered to patients. Unlike items, services do not have a stock quantity.

HAS RESULTS: This is a critical field. Check this box if the service produces a clinical result that needs to be recorded (e.g., a blood test, an x-ray, a vision test).

RESULT TEMPLATE: If a service "Has Results", you must provide a JSON template. This template defines the structure for entering the results later. For example, a blood pressure test might have a template like: 

{\n  "systolic": "",\n  "diastolic": ""\n}

Tip: Use a clear and consistent naming convention for your services, and group them into appropriate categories for easier management.
