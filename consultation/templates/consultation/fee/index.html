Consultation Fee Helper

Consultation fees are set based on the **specialization**, **patient category** (regular or insurance), and a specific **insurance plan** (if applicable).

Tip: You can define different fees for the same specialization if the patient is under a different insurance plan or is a regular patient.

The Validity (Days) field specifies how many days a paid consultation is valid for. For example, a value of '1' means it's valid for the day it's paid.

Note: A unique fee entry requires a combination of Specialization, Patient Category, and Insurance (Insurance can be left blank for 'Regular Patients').
